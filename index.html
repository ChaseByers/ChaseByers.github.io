The Gallery



This space has been created as an alternative to a formal gallery and draws inspiration from a
childhood matching game.


To explore the gallery please select a cement object.
The peice will be revealed and leave your memory and imagination to choose it's partner.
Once you have found each of the matching pairs a collage of some of the creators favorites will be displayed.


Our intention is to make the work more impactful and instill its signifance deeper than
if you had only given each piece a quick glance. - Narendra Modi
